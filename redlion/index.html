A stunningly well renovated old pub in the well-established Church street in Stoke Newington truly has a lot to offer in a more quite part of town. There is an extensive selection of craft beers on the pumps and from the fridge with some notoriously delicious pizza’s made in house which feels more like eating in a restaurant rather than in a bar. At the ground floor you will find a GARLANDO Coperto glass top foosball machine with telescopic rods and integrated LED lights. The venue has a regular and local player base, so there is always a good chance to find new people to play with. The foosball accepts £1 coins. Red lion is available for group bookings and private hire.
